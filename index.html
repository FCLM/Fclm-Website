History

We were once a small active group running the [BTMF] outfit since Planetside 2 began in November 2012. With an inactive leader, we voted to split off under a new name with the purpose of creating an outfit with an active leadership. Todays active leadership includes Mono, Fappy and Spudles. Other leaders of FCLM have included DropbearOwnage, EatenAlive and Acherlon.

      Recruitment

      Want to wear the [FCLM] Tag and fight with us? Great! We do however have a few requirements

      • Jump on Teamspeak3, talk and play with us first. Lets see if you will enjoy your time with us
      • We'd really like it if you were above battle rank 30. It shows us that you have spent a bit of time in game and understand how things work
      • Be active, we are kicking players who are inactive for more than 3 months
      • We want mature members, preferably age 16+. There are exceptions to the rule providing we think you are a good fit for the outfit
      • If you are not 'maining' with us, let us know. We are happy to have alts in our outfit but we would still love it if you still slightly active with us
      • Outfit ops run on a Sunday night, it would be a good idea to attend one ops night before requesting to join us
      • Contact either M0N0, FappyMcFapper5 or Spudles in game if you are interested
